Help & Support

How do I add a new transaction?
Go to the "Add Transaction" page, fill in the details, and click "Add".
Can I set a budget for a category?
Yes, visit the "Budget Planner" page and create or edit budgets for each category.
How can I track my savings goals?
Check the "Savings Goals" page to see your progress for each goal.
How do I change my account settings?
Visit the "Settings" page to update your profile, enable notifications, or logout.
